Choose Your Plant

Golden pothos
พลูด่าง
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT low light, but grows well in medium and higher light areas too!
WATER 50% of soil is dry
FOODThe Golden Pothos is such an easy indoor plant, and you don’t need to worry about fertilizing this one a lot.
Cactus
กระบองเพชร
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Cactus needs as much sunlight
WATER Water your Cactus sparingly, almost to the point of when the plant begins to wrinkle.
FOOD Before applying any type of plant food, make sure the soil is already damp-never apply to dry soil.
Monstera
มอนสเตอร่า
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Monstera can grow just about anywhere in your home!
WATER Water your Monstera when the top 50-75% of the soil is dry.
FOODFor best results, feed your plant once a month throughout the spring and summer.
Snake Plant
ลิ้นมังกร
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT They prefer bright indirect light where they will flourish.
WATER Water well, but do not allow the plants to become soggy.
FOOD Fertilize up to twice a month in the spring and summer, however, avoid over-fertilization, which can lead to brown leaf tips.
Ficus annulata
ช้อนเงินช้อนทอง
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Ficus prefers bright indirect light. Direct sun may burn the leaves and cause them to drop.
WATER Water when the top 50%-75% of the soil is dry.
FOOD Feed once a month during the spring and summer with a liquid fertilizer for indoor plants.
Rubber Plant
ยางอินเดีย
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Thrives in medium to bright indirect light, and can tolerate bright direct light.
WATER Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings.
FOOD For best results, feed your plant once during the spring and monthly throughout the summer.
Fiddle Fig
ไทรใบสัก
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Fiddle Leaf Fig will grow best with consistent, bright, filtered light.
WATER Water when the top 50%-75% of the soil becomes dry.
FOOD For best results, feed your plant once during the spring and monthly throughout the summer.
Philodendron
ฟิโลเดนดรอน
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Philodendron prefers bright, indirect light. It can survive in medium light conditions, but growth may slow.
WATER Water your Philodendron when the top 50-75% of soil is dry.
FOOD Feed your Philodendron Birkin monthly in the spring and summer with a basic houseplant food
Xanadu
ซานาดู
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Best in moderate to bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight as this may cause the leaves to fade.
WATER Water well then allow the top half of the soil to dry out before re-watering.
FOOD Apply a weak solution of liquid fertiliser two or three times during the growing season.
Boston fern
บอสตันเฟิร์น
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Boston Fern will thrive in low to bright indirect light.
WATER Water your Button Fern when the top inch of the soil becomes dry.
FOOD For best results, use a general-purpose liquid houseplant fertilizer diluted.
Aglaonema
เขียวหมื่นปี
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Bright indirect sunlight is optimal and will help your Aglaonema produce the beautiful pink spots on its leaves.
WATER Aglaonema prefer to be kept moist during the spring and summer, but make sure the soil isn’t soggy.
FOOD For best results, use a general houseplant fertilizer once a month during the spring and summer.
Ivy
ไอวี่
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT In both winter and summer, Ivy requires bright light.
WATER Let the top of the soil dry to the touch between wateringsใ
FOOD Fertilize your ivy about once a month in the spring, summer, and fall.
Alocasia
แก้วสารพัดนึก
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Alocasia will thrive in bright indirect light.
WATER Maintain a regular watering schedule and keep the soil of your plant moist, but not wet or saturated.
FOOD For best results, use a general houseplant fertilizer every four weeks during the spring and summer.
Mother of Thousands

Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Kept in bright filtered sunlight,
WATER Watered occasionally. Being a type of succulent
FOOD The plant thrives when growing in well-draining potting soil.
Zanzibar Gem
กวักมรกต
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT ZZ plants do best in moderate to bright indirect light, but will do fine in extremely low light areas.
WATER The ZZ Plant is like a cactus in that it needs less, rather than more, water.
FOOD Fertilize your ZZ twice a year with a general purpose plant food.
Croton
โกสน
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Croton will do best in a bright spot, as it needs lots of light (if possible 4-6 hrs. Per day) to produce all those colorful leaves!
WATER When the soil begins to dry out, water your plant thoroughly until the water flows out the bottom of the container.
FOOD For best results, use a general houseplant fertilizer once in early spring, once in early summer, and again in mid-summer. Do not feed in the fall or winter.
Spider Ivy / Spider Plant
ว่านเศรษฐีเรือนใน
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Spider Plant will tolerate lower light conditions, however, they prefer bright indirect light where they will flourish.
WATER Water well, but do not allow the plants to become soggy, which can lead to root rot.
FOOD Fertilize up to twice a month in the spring and summer.
Cape of Good Hope / Dracaena
วาสนา
Detail
How to take care
LIGHT Your Colorama prefers medium indirect sunlight.
WATER Overwatering causes root rot and is the main reason a Colorama plant dies.
FOOD This plant likes to be fed every two weeks in the spring and summer with a well-balanced plant food.
